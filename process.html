How We Turn Scrap into Cash — in Three Unbreakable Steps

At Demo Yard we’ve boiled the entire experience down to a process you can set your watch by. No paperwork marathons, no guessing games—just straight‑line efficiency from the minute you call to the moment cash hits your pocket.

Lock‑In Pricing

  • Call, text, or hit “Request a Quote.” We confirm today’s market price and reserve it for you—no surprise downgrades at the scale.
  • Need a roll‑off? Our dispatcher schedules delivery within 24 hours.
  • Every quote arrives with a digital ticket so you know the exact grade, weight, and rate before a single pound moves.

Roll In & Weigh Up

  • Pull onto our state‑certified scales; attendants guide you from start to finish.
  • A live display shows gross, tare, and net weights in real time—scan the screen, snap a photo if you like.
  • Forklifts and magnets unload your material fast, keeping you in the cab and off your clock.

Cash Out & Recycle Right

  • We print your finalized ticket and trigger payment on the spot—check, ACH, or payment card.
  • Your scrap heads straight to our shred, shear, or baler lines, where we recover 99 % of recyclable metal and divert the rest from landfill.
  • Need certificates of destruction or downstream traceability? They’re in your inbox before you’re back on the road.

Total yard time for most loads: under 15 minutes. Total turnaround from quote to cash: under 24 hours.
