Process: From Pull-Up to Payout

Our Promise

“Ten minutes in, cash (or ACH) out.”

We’ve spent five decades refining a system that keeps traffic moving, prices transparent, and paperwork painless. Whether you’re a first-timer with two bags of cans or an industrial hauler with 20 tons of Z-Twitch, the three steps below never change—only the scale ticket does.

1 Check Your Material — Know Before You Roll

Accepted for Top Dollar

  • Copper & Brass – bare bright, #1, #2, radiators, red brass fittings
  • Aluminum – sheet, cast, extrusions, wheels, siding, beverage cans
  • Steel & Iron – appliances (de-fluids drained), plate & structural, farm scrap
  • Stainless Steel – 304 & 316 solids, turnings, clean tanks
  • Catalytic Converters – OEM, aftermarket, small engine
  • E-Scrap & Batteries – servers, motherboards, lead-acid, lithium-ion (tape terminals)

Full list & prep guide — Download the Accepted Materials PDF for weight deductions, contamination rules, and photos of what not to bring.

Have mixed loads? No worries—our crew will sort and grade for you while you watch.

Not Accepted (for your safety & ours)

  • Pressurized cylinders, sealed drums, or tanks
  • Tires, liquids, or haz-mat (fuel, oil, paint)
  • Radioactive or biomedical waste

Need clarification? Hit the chat bubble or call (555) 123-SCRAP—real humans pick up.

2 Drive On & Unload — Fast Lanes, Pro Crew

Arrive & Weigh-In

  • Pull onto the certified inbound truck scale.
  • Get a printed inbound weight ticket—this locks your tare weight.

Follow the Color-Coded Lanes

  • Orange = Ferrous
  • Blue = Non-ferrous
  • Green = Catalytic Converters & E-Scrap

(Swipe the Yard Map below if you’re on mobile.)

Unload & Grade

  • Our team operates forklifts, magnets, and grapple loaders so you don’t lift a thing.
  • Each category is graded in plain view; you’ll see the scale readout live.

Outbound Scale

  • Empty vehicle rolls back over the scale.
  • Final ticket prints automatically—weight, grade, price per pound, total due.

First-Timer Tip: Watch our 20-second Drone Fly-Over video (opens full-screen) so you know exactly where to turn, park, and exit.

How often do prices change?
Copper & aluminum adjust with the global markets, sometimes twice daily. Check the Today’s Prices link before you haul.

Can you destroy hard drives?
Absolutely. Data-destruction certificates available on request.
