Services

Three ways we turn scrap into cash—and keep your project on schedule.

1 Ferrous & Non-Ferrous Buying

Fast weights, fair rates, instant payouts.

  • Metals we pay top dollar for: copper (all grades), brass, aluminum (sheet, extrusions, wheels), steel & cast iron, stainless, lead, zinc plus high-value alloys (nickel, titanium, Inconel, Monel).
  • Digital price board tied to LME & Comex—no hidden spreads.
  • Calibrated drive-on scale with printed tickets in plain view.
  • Cash, ACH, or check while you watch the ticket print.
  • Chain-of-custody option for exotic alloys—sealed totes, bar-coded tags, and certified XRF/LIBS reports.

2 Industrial Container Service (Roll-Offs & Luggers)

Bin swaps that never slow your line.

  • 20–60 yd³ bins matched to your scrap volume.
  • Same-day drop & swap inside a 50-mile radius (4-hr average).
  • Transparent tonnage reports—scale tickets & photos emailed before the truck leaves.
  • No rental fees—you pay freight only; metal value offsets the rest.
  • OSHA-certified drivers, GPS-tracked and fully insured.

3 Demolition & Clean-Up

From smokestacks to shop floors—we dismantle, segregate, and recycle.

  • Licensed & insured crews with confined-space & torch-cutting certs.
  • Turn-key project management—permits, utility disconnects, environmental surveys, final grading.
  • On-site processing (shears, portable balers, magnets) to maximize scrap value and minimize haul-offs.
  • 100% recycling goal—metals to the mill, concrete to aggregate, zero unsorted landfill loads.
